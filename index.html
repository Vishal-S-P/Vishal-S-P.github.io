Vishal Purohit

ECE Ph.D. Student, Purdue University

Hi! I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in the Department of ECE at Purdue University, where I work with Prof. Qiang Qiu. My research aims to develop novel methods and algorithms for learning high-dimensional signals from sparse and quantized measurements, with applications in computer vision.

Before joining Purdue, I was a research intern at IIT Varanasi for a year, where I worked on image processing and machine learning projects. Previously, I worked as an engineer in the medical electronics division at Tata Elxsi for 2 years, where I developed software and hardware solutions for medical devices.My research interest is computer vision with a focus on compressive sensing, generative modeling, object detection, and universal image segmentation. I am passionate about creating efficient and robust models that can handle real-world challenges and data. Apart from my research, I am also leading the development of state-of-the-art computer vision models for Google TensorFlow Model Garden, a repository of high-quality implementations of popular deep learning models under guidence of Prof. James Davis and Dr. Abdullah Rashwan (Google ). I am really fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Stanley Chan and Prof. Qi Guo.
