Vishal Purohit

Ph.D. Student, Purdue

I am currently a 2nd year Ph.D. student in the Department of ECE at Purdue University, where I work with Prof. Qiang Qiu. My research aims to develop novel methods and algorithms for learning and reconstructing high-dimensional signals from sparse and quantized measurements, with applications in computer vision. Before joining Purdue, I was research intern at IIT Varanasi for a year. Previously, I worked in medical electronics division as enginner at Tata Elxsi Limited for 2 years.

My research interest is computer vision with focuses on compressive sensing, generative modeling, object detection, and universal segmentation. Apart from my research, I am spearheading the development of state-of-the-art computer vision models for Google TensorFlow Model Garden, a repository of high-quality implementations of popular deep learning models under guidence of Prof. James Davis and Dr. Abdullah Rashwan I am really fortunate to collaborate with Prof. Stanley Chan and Prof. Qi Guo .
